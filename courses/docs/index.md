## Description

How do visual and sonic environments shape the way we experience space? This
course introduces computational methodologies for studying and designing
*mediated atmospheres*: processing multimodal data from built and urban
environments, interpreting them through neural representations, and applying
algorithmic approaches to speculative composition. Students will work
hands-on with audiovisual datasets, sonification and visualization pipelines,
and generative models, culminating in a final project that augments the
experiential dimension of a real place.
