# Agora

The open floor of Module 2. Post questions, share references, respond to each
other's proposals, and leave feedback after critique sessions. Comments are
powered by [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/youngjuene/youngjuene.github.io/discussions)
— sign in with your GitHub account to write; reactions work too.

!!! note "House rules"
    - Critique the work, not the person; be concrete about what you saw and heard.
    - Share references generously — a link with one sentence of context beats ten links without.
    - Questions are contributions. "I don't understand X" is always welcome.
